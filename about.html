my name is sarah murphy, and i love traveling!

by day, i'm a hard-working employee working to pay my bills. by night, i'm an avid traveler exploring the culture and environment around me. i'm always looking for new adventures!

ever since i was a little kid, i've always wanted to travel the world! luckily for me, as i've grown older, i've had many chances and opportunities to fly around the world. here, i share all of the trips i've been on, in hopes of spreading my love for traveling to others!

i've been to places like paris and cairo, where i, along with my friends, explore the city and its culture! as i visit more and more places, my understanding of different cultures has grown, as well as my fascination for them!
